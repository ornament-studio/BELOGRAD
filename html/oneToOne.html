Program

Original formula of training: 100% hands-on. Proved clinical results by colleagues attended this course.

MODULE 1

Mastering tooth preparation for veneers, crowns, onlays

Instead of 1000 words you will get 25 real clinical situations for practicing and improve skills. All that was collected during years you will pass only within 3 days. Original formula of training: 10% theory 90% practice.

MODULE 2

All about composite restorations and RUBBERDAMology

Would you like to match the color and do less occlusal corrections? To get perfect contact points and fit? To achieve aesthetic results and to learn how to perform great modeling? Then this course is for you! All modern concepts and schools are collected here.

MODULE 3

All about veneers.
Full course from smile design to bonding

Aesthetics from smile design to veneer bonding. On this course you will get what is taught on 6 separate programs. What does it mean? Your time will be used with maximum efficiency and you will have only what is really needed for practice.
