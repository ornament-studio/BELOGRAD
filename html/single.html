Double loop technique

Double loop technique is considered to be one of the most efficient methods to isolate cervical zones. In many of cases it allows to work without clamp which gives more space and freedom to operator. Loop is placed around the tooth and tightened. As you may see – here we were able to open margin and cervical area completely.
